I am an undergraduate at Princeton University studying Electrical and Computer Engineering, with a strong passion for computational biology and the broader application of technology to biological systems. My research interests center on leveraging biological mechanisms to replicate and illuminate real-world, applicable technologies. At the Atkinson Lab, I hope to pursue this intersection by investigating conductance measurement through interdigitated electrodes and exploring the design of such electrodes using anode-respiring bacteria. Outside the lab and classroom, I enjoy playing and watching sports, discovering new foods, and exploring new places.
