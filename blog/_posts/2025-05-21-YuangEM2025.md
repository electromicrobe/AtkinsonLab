Yuang Guo (CEE - G1) traveled to Aarhus, Denmark to attend the Early Career Scientist Workshop and present on our work on cable bacteria at the [Electromicrobiology 2025 meeting](https://conferences.au.dk/electromicrobiology-2025). He had the chance to meet and learn how to work with cable bacteria from our collaborators at the [Center for Electromicrobiology](https://bio.au.dk/forskning/forskningscentre/center-for-elektromikrobiologi-cem)
